About Ironhaven
Ironhaven is a prosperous mining town nestled in the foothills of the Stonecrest Mountains. Known for its skilled blacksmiths and abundant iron deposits, the town has grown wealthy from trade with neighboring kingdoms. The Rusty Anvil Tavern serves as the social heart of the community, where miners, merchants, and adventurers gather to share stories and conduct business.
Notable Residents
Thora Ironheart is the town's master blacksmith, renowned throughout the region for her exceptional craftsmanship. Her workshop produces some of the finest weapons and armor in the land.
Eldrin the Sage is a mysterious wizard who arrived in town several years ago. He is often seen studying ancient texts and is known to possess knowledge of forgotten magic.
